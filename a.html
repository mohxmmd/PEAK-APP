Welcome to MES College of Architecture Kakkodi, Kozhikode

MES College of Architecture, Kakkodi, Kozhikode was started in the academic year 2013-14 with an intake of 40 students. The need for an Architecture College of excellence in this part of Kerala has been felt by us for a long time based on tremendous demand for admission at the School of Architecture in the MES College of Engineering, MESCE, Kuttippuram. MES now wish to extend its services to the people in the northern parts of the state for their convenience, offering excellent education and training in Architecture.

The MES College of Architecture, Kakkodi, Kozhikode is situated atop Nedumalakkunnu, Kakkodi, in the outskirts, 12km away from Calicut city. The College campus site extents to 12 acres of land and render itself for further expansion and development. Residential facility for students and staff would be developed soon and it will create an ambience for an exciting working environment for creativity. The College is situated in a beautiful natural setting to be a perfect campus for Architects.

Recognized by the CoA and the Government of Kerala, the College is affiliated to the University of Calicut. The College is easily accessible by road and rail, being only 12km from Kozhikode railway station. The nearest Airport is at Karippur, 38 km away from the College.
